Interesting Facts About Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of the communication amoung chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensive tail that they use to wraparound the tree branches.

Interesting Facts About Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts About Camel

  • Camels have a double row of very long eyelashes to protect the eye from blowing sand.
  • Camels are even-toed ungulates that have one or two humps.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.

    Interesting Facts About Panda

  • In captivity, the average lifespan of a panda is 30 years.
  • Pandas reside in forests that are rich in coniferous leaves and dense under-stories of bamboo.
  • Pandas have a distinct black and white coat that is dense and thick to protect against cooler climates.
